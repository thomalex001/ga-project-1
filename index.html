// GAME RULES

  • A squadron of Wario fighters are ascending on Mario World...
  • You are the only one who can stop them!!!
  • Move Mario left: ← or right: →
  • Shoot fireballs at the Wario fighters with the space bar.
  • Each Wario fighters killed will get you 100pts!
  • Destroy ALL Wario fighters to win the game!
  • Make sure not to get hit by Spinies thrown at you or you will lose a life.
  • If you lose 3 lives or if the Wario fighters land on Mario World.. it's GAMEOVER!
